About Me

It’s funny how some of my biggest hobbies became some of my biggest and endless projects. As a six year old I was watching Pokemon, Dragonball and Yuyu Hakusho(my all time fav). I was the kid that ran around doing actual anime moves and speaking like a character in real life. It didn't stop there though. I began to watch my anime in Japanese dub with English subtitles, play my games that way and strictly buy anime games. I was so engulfed in Japanese media so much that I started to listen to the opening and ending songs of my favorite anime. Boom, a monster was born! I started to listen to so many japanese bands and soundtracks from movies and shows that I fell in this deep love with music also. The chords they played in these diverse soundtracks and songs captivated me to the point where I started trying to make my own music. Twelve to thirteen years later and now I am making music that past me would be proud of and reading and watching shows in the language that made me fall in love with all my big passions. Looking back I realize that if I want something, I will make it a reality. I am super dedicated and have a passion for learning new skills. These uncomfortable experiences shaped me to learn to adapt to being uncomfortable and making those spaces the most comfortable to be in. I love to strive where the odds are against me.
